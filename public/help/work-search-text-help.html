Work Search Text

The characters ":" and "@" have special meanings. Leave them out of your search or you will get unexpected results.

*: any characters book* will find book and books and booking.

space: a space acts like AND Harry Potter will find Harry Potter and Harry James Potter but not Harry.

|: OR (not exclusive) Harry | Potter will find Harry, Harry Potter, and Potter.

": words in exact sequence "Harry Lockhart" will find "Harry Lockhart" but not Harry Potter/Gilderoy Lockhart.

-: NOT Harry -Lockhart will find Harry Potter but not Harry Lockhart or Gilderoy Lockhart/Harry Potter.
