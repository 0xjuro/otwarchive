Potential Givers

Potential givers are listed here (and in the pull-down menu) in order of "match quality" -- that is, people who are a better match are listed ahead of those who are worse matches. You can click the potential giver's name to see details on the match between them and the recipient.

Not everyone can be assigned their best match, but the matching process tries to assign everyone the best match possible, and while avoiding circular matching where possible.
